About MK II

MK II is an FTC team made up of middle and high school students in Fremont, CA. We were founded in 2024 and are excited to start competing in the First Tech Challenge as a rookie team this year. Our team mission is to help spread FIRST and STEM in under-resourced communities in order to ensure that other people can enjoy robotics as much as we do!

We created this website for many reasons, including helping teams get items quickly instead of waiting for long shipping times, and by helping teams to sell items instead of returning them with a restock fee, helping them get most of their money back.

For more information, visit our website: https://sites.google.com/view/mk-ii/home
